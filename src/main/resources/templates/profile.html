Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add new products?
Go to the Products tab in the sidebar and click on "Add New Product". Fill out the required fields and save.
How can I view inventory levels?
Navigate to the Stock section. It shows current stock quantity for all products.
Can I download monthly sales reports?
Yes, go to the Reports section and choose the "Sales Report". You can download it in PDF or Excel format.
